Families are moving towards the trend of depicting their family-style through photography. In the past, people just used to go to photographers for conventional poses. But now, people like custom photography to capture memorable moments.  Every family has a story, memories, and special moments. They want to capture those special moments in a casual way. The interest of people has deviated towards showing how they live. We can say this trend in modern family photography. The trend of photography has prevailed to the extent that people first plan for photography and poses before planning a trip. The Gill Family session at Edmonton River valley park made me learn some extraordinary things about any family structure. Before sharing their cute moment photos, I will highlight the latest trends in modern photography.

## **Latest trends of modern photography **

When I was a child, my mother asked me to see in a camera and smile while photographing. But now the latest trend of contemporary photography has shifted towards the candid moments. It is good, as more the natural you will pose the more the best photo will capture. According to my experience, deviation from fake and alert poses has gifted us the memorable moments. To pose in casual jeans shirt while smiling and running with parents give pure and perfect poses. The urban environments, open fields, and the home have become the best places to capture family moments.

### **The best things about Gill Family session at Edmonton River valley park **

Azin and Donny were always a fan of my work. Today it was finally the day we were able to do the family session in Edmonton around the [River Valley area](https://www.edmonton.ca/activities_parks_recreation/parks_rivervalley/river-valley-parks.aspx). Let’s start with the little kid; he was full of energy and character, always curious about what’s happening around him. His famous hand gesture was pointing the finger at me, wondering, what I was doing. Yes, the candid photos which this family gave me were all because they posed naturally.

Moreover, the place which they chose for family photography was the best choice.  The green paths, sunshine, and flowers added colors to their photos. They enjoyed the photography session as a weekend trip.
