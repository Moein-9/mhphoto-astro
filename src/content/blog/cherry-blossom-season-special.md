Cherry blossom season is around the corner in [Edmonton](https://exploreedmonton.com/), and with that, my special is back!

**This special is ideal for Families, engagement, maternity.  **

This special will be valid from Saturday, May 27th until June 10th, when the blossoms are at their peak around the [University of Alberta](https://www.ualberta.ca/) campus and other city parks.


FAQ:

How can I book with you?

Simply, email me at Moein@mhphoto.ca

How much is the fee?

The fee it's $125,  in order to hold your spot, a non-refundable down payment of $75 is required to hold your spot.

What if on the day of my session, the cherry blossoms were not blooming?

That would suck, but we have two options, we can either reschedule the session to further date, or we can continue with the session.

What if I decided to cancel?

I am sorry to hear that, but you are more than welcome to reschedule to any other date that works for you. However, if you decided not to do the session, your down payment is non-refundable.

Do you have any locations that have cherry blossom trees?

Yes i do! Many of Edmonton's best blossom trees are along the [river valley](https://www.edmonton.ca/activities_parks_recreation/parks_rivervalley/river-valley) parks.
