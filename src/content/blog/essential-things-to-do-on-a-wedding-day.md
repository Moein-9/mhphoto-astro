## List of all essential things to do on a wedding day

Today, we have gathered all these essential things that can improve your wedding day and makes it unique. Here is the list of Essential Things to do on a wedding day:

### **Guests**

Guests are a crucial factor on a wedding to make it unique or chaotic. You should always choose the guests who should be invited and also estimate the total number of guests. It will help you in arranging the number of tables and chairs in the wedding hall. It also helps to know how much the wedding hall should extend. The right arrangement will allow the guests to ease, adjust, and roam easily in the lobby.

### **Wedding Dresses**

Wedding dresses are the essential thing of a wedding day that you should plan or buy with great care. If both the groom and bride look beautiful, they will feel confident. They will feel leisure in interacting with guests and in performing other rituals of the wedding day. If the bide is concerned with her bridal dress, then the man should allow her to choose her dress for a wedding. So, the choice of both the bride and groom should be kept in mind so that they are happy with everything about their marriage.

### **Meal Plan**

Meals served at the wedding also have a significant effect on the mood of all the guests. You should manage both light and another kind of meals so that everyone at the wedding has their desired food. But you should also control that the expense of the meal must be as least as possible so that you can meet the other costs of your wedding as well. This task achieved by arranging the required amount of food for the total guests you have invited. In this way, no food can go into waste.
