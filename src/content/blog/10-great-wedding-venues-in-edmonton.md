For your wedding occasion in Edmonton, you need to find the perfect venue to make the most out of your special day. There is an extensive range of venues ranging from fairytale castle, rustic country barn to an elegant banquet hall. So, all you need to do is attain the information regarding the prices and availability. Right after that, plan the ceremony and attend the reception accordingly. Here is the list of the ten Great Wedding Venues in Edmonton

### Radisson hotel and convention centre Edmonton

This would be the right venue for making your special day magical. Wedding in hotels basically brings everything under one roof. In this way, you don't have to worry about the details. The 14000 square feet space with two grand ballrooms that can accommodate 450 people. The presence of the side patio and parking spots makes it a convenient place for your wedding.

### Mirage Banquet Hall:

From perfect orchestration to the celebration, Mirage Banquet Hall is the perfect place for your [wedding event](https://mhphoto.ca/). Choose the size, theme, and style of your wedding along with the entertainment style. Floor plans are available upon request according to the requirement of the layout.

### Delta Hotels by Marriott: 

[Delta Hotels by Marriott Edmonton](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/yegde-delta-hotels-edmonton-centre-suites/) is a venue that provides breathtaking views, best amenities, and great facilities. This hotel provides an excellent ambience to celebrate your big day. With around 13 event rooms and 25,000 square feet of reception space, this classy hotel can accommodate up to 1650 guests.

### [Fairmont Hotel Macdonald](https://www.fairmont.com/macdonald-edmonton/):

This fairytale venue is perfect for attaining an unparalleled view of the North Saskatchewan River Valley. The presence of the extravagant ballrooms, romantic architecture, crystal chandeliers, and soaring ceilings make it a perfect backdrop for your big day. 

### Fort Edmonton Park: 

[Fort Edmonton Park](https://www.fortedmontonpark.ca/) is located in Edmonton is a venue that specializes in hosting special events surrounded by living history. This historic site offers aesthetic ambience with two historic churches and memorial garden to make your big day more happening. 

### Pine and Pond:

[Pine and Pond](https://pineandpond.ca/) is a premier outdoor wedding venue located near Ponoka, Alberta. This venue offers a pond, a log cabin, a fountain, manicured lawns, and waterfalls that are surrounded by mature trees. 

## Holiday Inn Express Edmonton Downtown:

Located in Alberta, this one place has the perfect setting, high-end amenities, flexible tooms, and experienced staff to make your event seamless and classy. With the stunning view of the river valley and downtown core, it can accommodate 100 guests.

### Majestic Palace:

The majestic palace is a state of the art venue located in Edmonton that is fully equipped banquet hall facilities. From fancy, elegant, to modern, every wedding and theme is catered with attention in this 6695 square feet space of the Oasis and Granada Ballrooms.

### The Whitewood Barn:

The Whitewood Barn provides an ever-lasting memory for your big day. This rustic and elegant 2400 square foot barn provides a hidden away wedding ceremony area with a cosy outdoor area and a private bridal suite. 

### Italian Culture Center:

This place is fully equipped with in house team of coordinators and planners. The experienced wedding coordinator and banquet staff will assist in executing your wedding flawlessly. By accommodating over 150 guests, it offers five different indoor and outdoor spaces to cater to your preferences.
